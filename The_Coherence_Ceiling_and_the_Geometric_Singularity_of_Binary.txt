  K = √2 × (1/√2) = 1.000

  ln(2)   ≈ 0.6931471806
  1/√2    ≈ 0.7071067812
  
  Shortfall:  0.7071 − 0.6931 = 0.0140
  Relative:   0.0140 / 0.7071 = 1.9742%

Therefore:

  K_AUD = √2 × ln(2) = 0.9802581435

(continuous, geometric: √2) and transcendental necessity (discrete,
